# Behavioral Attribution Model (BAM) – Ransomware Research

**Focus:** Criminological and behavioral attribution framework for ransomware ecosystems, with emphasis on state-linked and state-tolerated threat actors.

## What this demonstrates

- Development of a criminological attribution framework analyzing ransomware ecosystem persistence and state-linked actors, bridging behavioral science and cyber threat intelligence.
- Design of a Bayesian Attribution Score (BAS) model to inform cyber attribution assessments and sanctions recommendations.
- Introduction of “functional enablement” as a supplementary attribution standard to address gaps in existing legal and doctrinal frameworks (e.g., Tallinn Manual 2.0).
